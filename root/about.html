About

Josh Berg has always wanted to be an inventor, someone who makes fantastic things for people. In Fall 2020, Josh will graduate from San Francisco State University as an industrial design major, the closest title he could get to "inventor." Josh enjoys collaborating with his fellow designers to come up with the best ideas possible. In 2018 has part of a team he won Best Social Impact design at Swissnex Designathon. He excels at using many iterations of sketches at physical prototypes to make sure users' needs are met. Josh also loves to travel; it allows him to go somewhere new and met and learn from new people. He is always looking for the next adventure.
